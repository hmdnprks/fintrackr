## Introducing Fintrackr

**Fintrackr is a personal finance dashboard built specifically for Indonesian bank statements.**

You drop your Mandiri PDF e-statement in. Fintrackr reads it, categorises every transaction using 50+ Indonesian merchant rules plus AI that understands local context — GoPay, Alfamart, PLN, KAI, Tokopedia, your KKB installment, your BPJS. All of it.

In minutes, you go from a wall of cryptic codes to a clear picture of your financial life.

## Your story, in four tabs

### Where did the money go? — *Overview*

A heat map calendar shows exactly which days you spent the most. That suspicious Friday the 3rd? Click it. Every transaction, timestamped. No more guessing.

Month-over-month comparison tells you: your Food & Dining spend jumped 40% last month. Was it that birthday dinner? Or have you been quietly ordering GoFood every other night?

### Am I on track? — *Insights*

Your **Financial Health Score** — a single number from 0 to 100 — tells you where you stand across four pillars: savings rate, emergency fund, investment rate, and budget discipline. Grade A means you're crushing it. Grade D means there's work to do. Either way, you know.

The **50/30/20 breakdown** shows you honestly: how much of your income goes to needs, wants, and surplus. No judgement. Just clarity.

And if you've ever wondered about **FIRE** — Financial Independence, Retire Early — Fintrackr tells you your number. With your birth year, it calculates exactly what age you could stop working if you keep saving at your current rate. 35? 45? 55? Now you know what you're working toward.

### Am I staying within limits? — *Budget*

Set monthly limits for each category. Watch the bars fill up in real time. Green is safe. Amber is a warning. Red means you've already blown it.

Your recurring commitments — Netflix, Indihome, BPJS, KPR — are auto-detected and listed separately. These are your fixed costs. Everything above them is where you have choices.

### What do I have? — *Assets*

Track your savings accounts, gold, investments, and goal pockets in one place. See your net worth grow over time. Know your emergency fund tier — Critical, Low, Building, Healthy, or Strong — with Indonesian-context advice at each level.

Got THR coming? Fintrackr's **Windfall Allocation** AI gives you a specific plan: put this much toward your emergency fund gap, this much toward your investment target, this much toward your goals — and yes, keep a slice as reward. You earned it.

## Your data never leaves your device.

We mean this literally. There is no server. No database. No account to create. No cloud sync.

Your financial data is encrypted on your phone or laptop using AES-256 — the same standard banks use — and stays there. When you close the app, it locks. Nobody can access it, not even us, because *we don't exist in this equation*.

AI features (categorisation, insights) are opt-in. You bring your own API key. Your transaction descriptions go to the AI provider you choose — we never touch them.

This is what privacy-first actually means.
